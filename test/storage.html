These pages provide some examples of how to use Awac. Most pages includes a link to view the page outside of the app.

In Awac actions is a general term for events generated by the menu items whether in nav drawer, the action bar or the options menu.

Actions have a display lable and associated 'action' string. When one of these menu items is selected the registered on-action handler is called.
